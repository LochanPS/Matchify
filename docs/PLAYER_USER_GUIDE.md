## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### 1. Can't Find Tournaments in My City
**Solution:**
- Check your profile city setting
- Try nearby cities (tournaments often accept players from surrounding areas)
- Contact organizers directly if you're willing to travel

#### 2. Payment Failed But Money Deducted
**Solution:**
- Check your bank statement (amount may be on hold)
- Contact our support with transaction details
- Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days

#### 3. Registered But Not Receiving Updates
**Solution:**
- Check email spam/junk folder
- Verify phone number in profile
- Enable app notifications in phone settings
- Contact tournament organizer directly

#### 4. Want to Cancel Registration
**Solution:**
- Contact tournament organizer before tournament starts
- Refund policy varies by organizer
- Some organizers allow transfers to other players

#### 5. Partner Didn't Show Up for Doubles
**Solution:**
- Inform tournament organizer immediately
- Some tournaments allow last-minute partner changes
- You may need to withdraw from that category

#### 6. Dispute with Match Result
**Solution:**
- Speak with tournament referee/organizer immediately
- Provide any evidence (photos, witness accounts)
- Most tournaments have an appeals process

## Tips for Success

### Before Tournaments
1. **Arrive Early**: Reach venue 30 minutes before your match
2. **Warm Up**: Use practice courts if available
3. **Bring Essentials**: Racket, shuttles, water, towel
4. **Check Weather**: For outdoor tournaments
5. **Emergency Contact**: Share tournament details with family

### During Tournaments
1. **Stay Hydrated**: Drink water between games
2. **Respect Opponents**: Good sportsmanship always
3. **Follow Rules**: Tournament referee's decision is final
4. **Support Others**: Cheer for fellow players
5. **Have Fun**: Remember why you love badminton!

### After Tournaments
1. **Update Profile**: Add any achievements or photos
2. **Connect with Players**: Make new badminton friends
3. **Provide Feedback**: Help improve future tournaments
4. **Plan Next Tournament**: Keep the momentum going!

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: Do I need to be an experienced player to join?**
A: No! Our platform welcomes players of all experience levels. Choose categories that match your comfort level.

**Q: Can I join tournaments in other cities?**
A: Yes, if the organizer allows. Check tournament details or contact the organizer.

**Q: What if I'm a complete beginner?**
A: Look for "Open" or "Recreational" categories, or contact organizers who often welcome new players.

### Registration & Payment

**Q: Can I register for multiple categories?**
A: Yes! You can join as many categories as you want in the same tournament.

**Q: Is my payment information secure?**
A: Yes, we use Razorpay's secure payment gateway. We never store your card details.

**Q: Can I get a refund if I can't attend?**
A: Refund policies vary by organizer. Contact them as early as possible.

### Matches & Results

**Q: How are matches scheduled?**
A: Organizers create match schedules and notify all players via email/SMS.

**Q: What if my opponent doesn't show up?**
A: You'll receive a walkover win. Inform the tournament organizer.

**Q: Can I request specific match timings?**
A: Contact the organizer, but accommodations depend on tournament logistics.
