## Platform Strategy Overview

### Primary Platforms & Objectives

#### Instagram (@pathfinder_badminton)
- **Primary Goal:** Visual storytelling and community building
- **Target Audience:** 18-35 year old badminton enthusiasts
- **Content Focus:** Tournament highlights, player stories, tips
- **Posting Frequency:** 1 post daily, 3-5 stories daily
- **Key Metrics:** Engagement rate, reach, story completion rate

#### Twitter/X (@pathfinderbdmt)
- **Primary Goal:** Real-time updates and community engagement
- **Target Audience:** Active players, organizers, sports enthusiasts
- **Content Focus:** Live updates, quick tips, community discussions
- **Posting Frequency:** 3-5 tweets daily
- **Key Metrics:** Engagement rate, retweets, mentions

#### Facebook (@PathfinderBadminton)
- **Primary Goal:** Community groups and event promotion
- **Target Audience:** 25-45 year old players and organizers
- **Content Focus:** Event promotion, community discussions, groups
- **Posting Frequency:** 1 post daily, active group management
- **Key Metrics:** Group growth, event responses, shares

#### LinkedIn (@pathfinder-sports)
- **Primary Goal:** B2B outreach and organizer acquisition
- **Target Audience:** Tournament organizers, sports facility owners
- **Content Focus:** Industry insights, organizer success stories
- **Posting Frequency:** 3-4 posts weekly
- **Key Metrics:** Connection growth, engagement, lead generation

#### YouTube (@PathfinderBadminton)
- **Primary Goal:** Educational content and tournament highlights
- **Target Audience:** Serious players seeking improvement
- **Content Focus:** Tutorials, tournament coverage, interviews
- **Posting Frequency:** 2-3 videos weekly
- **Key Metrics:** Watch time, subscribers, video completion rate

## Visual Content Guidelines

### Instagram Feed Aesthetic
- **Color Palette:** Blue (#3B82F6), Green (#10B981), Orange (#F59E0B)
- **Style:** Clean, modern, energetic
- **Grid Layout:** Mix of single posts, carousels, and video content
- **Consistency:** Maintain brand colors and fonts across all posts

### Story Templates
- Tournament countdown timers
- Registration reminders
- Behind-the-scenes content
- User-generated content frames
- Poll and question stickers
- Quick tips and facts
- Live tournament updates

### Video Content Standards
- **Aspect Ratios:** 
  - Instagram: 1:1 (feed), 9:16 (stories/reels)
  - YouTube: 16:9
  - Twitter: 16:9 or 1:1
- **Duration:**
  - Instagram Reels: 15-30 seconds
  - YouTube: 2-10 minutes
  - Twitter: 30-60 seconds
- **Quality:** Minimum 1080p, prefer 4K when possible
- **Branding:** Include logo and brand colors

## Community Management Guidelines

### Response Time Targets
- **Comments:** Within 2 hours during business hours
- **Direct Messages:** Within 1 hour during business hours
- **Mentions:** Within 30 minutes during business hours
- **Crisis Situations:** Within 15 minutes

### Tone of Voice
- **Friendly:** Approachable and welcoming
- **Enthusiastic:** Show passion for badminton
- **Helpful:** Always provide value and assistance
- **Professional:** Maintain credibility and expertise
- **Inclusive:** Welcome players of all levels

### Engagement Strategies
- Ask questions to encourage comments
- Create polls and interactive content
- Share user-generated content with credit
- Respond to comments with personalized messages
- Use emojis appropriately to add personality
- Tag relevant users and partners
- Cross-promote content across platforms

### Crisis Management Protocol
1. **Acknowledge:** Respond quickly to acknowledge the issue
2. **Investigate:** Gather facts before providing detailed response
3. **Communicate:** Provide transparent updates on resolution
4. **Follow-up:** Ensure issue is resolved to user satisfaction
5. **Learn:** Document lessons learned for future prevention
